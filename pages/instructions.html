Instructions for the LineSnap study:

You will see a series of 45 line charts. For each line charts, you will have 3 questions.


Your task involves 3 parts:

  1. Pattern Identification: Select the pattern that best matches the line chart from a set of five possible options. (10-second time limit)
  2. Confidence Rating: Indicate how confident you are in your selection. (10-second time limit)
  3. Reasoning: Provide a brief explanation of your reasoning in a free-response text box. (15-second time limit)


After selecting your answer, please click the "Next" button to proceed to the following question.
