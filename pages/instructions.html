Instructions for the LineSnap study:

In this study, you will be shown a total of 54 line charts. For each chart, you will complete three short tasks designed to assess how you interpret visual patterns in line charts.


Each chart interaction consists of the following three tasks:

  1. Pattern Identification (10 seconds) — You will select the pattern that best represents the line chart from five possible options.
  2. Confidence Rating (10 seconds) — You will indicate how confident you are in the pattern you selected using a 5-point scale.
  3. Reasoning (15 seconds) — You will provide a brief written explanation describing how you arrived at your answer.

Each task has a time limit, and the chart will advance automatically when time is up. You may also click the "Next" button to move forward sooner.


To familiarize yourself with the process, you may view a demo of the full interaction before starting the study. When you're ready, click "Begin Study" to start the experiment.
