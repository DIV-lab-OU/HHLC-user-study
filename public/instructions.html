Instructions for the HHLC User Study:

In this study, you will be shown 8 different visualizations representing various types of data representations: scatterplots, line charts, bar graphs, and maps—each in both single-class and multi-class forms. For each visualization, you will answer three questions designed to understand how you interpret and comprehend visual data representations.


For each visualization, you will be asked:

  1. Understanding Question — "What can you understand from the graph given?" Please describe in your own words what the visualization shows you.
  2. Factors Question — "What factors/features drive your understanding?" Please explain what specific elements, patterns, or features help you understand the data.
  3. Difficulty Question — "Your difficulty level in interpreting this graph?" Please rate from 1-5 (Very Easy to Very Difficult) and optionally elaborate on any challenges.

There are no time limits for this study. Please take your time to provide thoughtful responses. Your honest and detailed answers will help us understand how people with different backgrounds interpret visualizations.


When you're ready to begin, click "Start Study" below. The study should take approximately 15-20 minutes to complete.
